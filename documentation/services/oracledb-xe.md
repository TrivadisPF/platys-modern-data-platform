## How to enable?

```
platys init --enable-services ORACLE_XE
platys gen
```

### Connect through JDBC

* **JDBC Url:**  	`jdbc:oracle:thin:@dataplatform:1522/XEPDB1`
* **JDBC Driver Class Name:** 	`oracle.jdbc.driver.OracleDriver`
